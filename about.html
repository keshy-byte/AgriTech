    • About Us

    At Agri Tech, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural sector through sustainable practices and innovative technologies. Founded in 2024 by Mary Wangechi Kimaru, a Software Engineer with a deep passion for environmental sustainability and technological advancement in farming, Agri Tech aims to empower farmers and agricultural enthusiasts to adopt eco-friendly practices that benefit both the planet and their yields.

    Driven by the challenges faced by the agricultural community, including climate change, soil degradation, and the need for higher productivity,I envisioned a platform that not only educates but also provides practical solutions. Agri Tech is the culmination of years of research, hands-on experience, and the belief that technology and nature can work together harmoniously.

    Our mission is to create a sustainable farming future by promoting practices that enhance soil health, conserve water, and reduce the reliance on chemical pesticides and fertilizers. Through our website, we share insights on organic farming, precision agriculture, and the latest in agritech innovations, from drone farming to smart irrigation systems.

    Join us on this journey towards a greener future. Whether you are a seasoned farmer, an agri-tech enthusiast, or someone curious about the intersection of technology and agriculture, Agri Tech is your go-to source for information, inspiration, and innovation.

    The Creator

    Hello, I'm Mary Wangechi Kimaru, the founder of Agri Tech. My journey into the world of sustainable agriculture began during my time at Kirinyaga University, where I witnessed firsthand the impacts of conventional farming on our environment.

    In my quest, I've explored everything from traditional farming methods to cutting-edge technologies, always with sustainability in mind. Agri Tech is more than a website; it's a platform for change, designed to inspire and educate those who share our vision of a sustainable agricultural landscape enriched by technology.

    When I'm not researching the latest in sustainable farming or advocating for environmental stewardship, I enjoy Coding. I believe that understanding our connection to the land and to the food we eat is crucial in making meaningful changes in the world.
